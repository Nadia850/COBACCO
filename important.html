player of the week

I understand how to use modal verbs especially must,can and should. Where I found that we use must for obligation and it used like should, for example, you can say that” you must submit your work on time.” or you should submit your work on time. Though I understand how to use must,can and should. I don’ t understand how to use might be because I saw that it is some how the same as may be because both are used for guessing. In addition, I understand how to use aught because goes in line with proposition”to”.
