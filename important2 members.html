Background

This association was created after two people sat to gather thinking about global challenges on environmental management and biodiversity conservation, poor living standards of people as well as huge multitude of unemployed people especially young generations. From a long time, discussion, they have found that most of the global challenges are from Human pressures on natural resources to find resources for being fed.

After time they came up with an idea of creating a youth association which will operate in areas of high risk communities on poor livelihood especially near by a protected area where people are encroaching the resources from protected areas searching for food causing conflicts between protected areas management and local communities.
