Overview

COBACCO(Community Based-Conservation Cooperative) is a Youth Cooperative working within local communities acting as a social organization whose aims are to: integrate local communities into Biodiversity conservation through community based conservation, Environmental management and protection for sustainable development through climate change mitigation and Community development.


Background

This Cooperative was created after two people sat to gather thinking about global challenges on environmental management and biodiversity conservation, poor living standards of people as well as huge multitude of unemployed people especially young generations. From a long time, discussion, they have found that most of the global challenges are from Human pressures on natural resources to find resources for being fed,

increase in population rate pressing the resources and lead to resources over-exploitation and this increase has led to the resources competions and high competence on job opportunities hence unemployment noted. Not only that but also people are searching for solutions focusing on existing opportunities instead of discovering hidden opportunities, bringing innovation, creativity and job creation.

Who we are

we are youth association which operate in areas of high risk communities on poor livelihood especially near by a protected area where people are encroaching the resources from protected areas searching for food causing conflicts between protected areas management and local communities.

Not only this but also people in rural areas are the ones vulnerable on climate changes impacts and due to this, it becomes a big challenge to find alternative solutions mainly for youth where almost of literate youth are unemployed and lack of capital has become a big challenge for project creation and this undermine the development and brought about poor living conditions.
