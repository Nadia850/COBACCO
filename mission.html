Vision, Mission and Services

Vision

Engage in sustainable development our country Rwanda through empowering citizens from grass roots youth and young generation for full engagement in safe use of resources and existing opportunities.

Mission

To use our skills, knowledge and opportunities for Building a stable country socio-economically through safe use of resources by addressing the poverty eradication, strengthening youth capacities on seeking development and generally empowering local people
